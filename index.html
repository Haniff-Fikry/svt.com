About Us

Our story begins in May 26, 2015 whereby in the middle Seoul City, South Korea. Our Seventeen-Itaewon was founded by our Director, Choi Seungchol alongside with his 2 close friends, Yoon Jeonghan and Hong Jisoo which is our General Manager and First Assistant Manager. Our restaurant was first inspired by pure inspiration of giving the taste of heaven to people who visit our restaurant.

We started by renting a stall nearby the Seoul City to give people a taste of our cuisine. Surprisingly, people are loving our food and soon later the demand was bombarded. As years passed, we took a brave step to expand our brand. We started to recruit manpower which include Wen Junhui, Lee-Jihoon, Kim Wonwoo, Kwon Soonyoung, Lee Seokmin, Xu Minghao, Kim Mingyu, Boo Seungkwan, Choi Hansol and Lee Chan where they became part of our success. As our manpower increased, we began to expand our brand throughout the entire South Korea. It was a big success since people were loving our food. Year 2020, we started to go international as our company keep moving forward. Started to expand to China, followed by Japan and many other countries. Our aim is to keep giving people the taste of our heaven and make them satisfied with what they spend.
